Strawberry Lemonade

Description

Who doesn't love an ice cold glass of lemonade on a hot summer day? This recipe inludes just that with an extra strawberry flare.

Prepare this lemonade for you, friends, or even a lemonade stand - it's sure to be a crowd pleaser!

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Lemons
  • Sugar/Artificial Sweetener
  • Strawberries
  • Ice

Steps

  1. Use a juicer (or hand squeeze) lemons. Add them to a water base
  2. Squeeze strawberries into juice and add them to the mixture as well
  3. For a sweeter taste, include extra sugar or artificial sweetener
  4. Serve over ice and garnish with a lemon wedge and split strawberry!
