Oven Baked Chicken

Description

Look at all them chickens! Create a quick, easy, and delicious chicken breast with a few ingredients and a ready oven.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Olive Oil (or oil substatute)
  • Salt/Pepper/Seasonings

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F
  2. Start by preparing the chicken breast, cutting out any extra fat
  3. Cover bottom of deep pan with oil and add prepared chicken breast
  4. Season chicken to your liking with salt, pepper, and additional herbs/seasonings (I love to use freshly cut oregano and basil)
  5. Place in oven and bake for 25 minutes
  6. Check to see if center is cooked - if not, let bake for 5 more minutes
  7. When cooked through, remove from ove, let cool, plate, and enjoy!
