Pizza

Description

This homemade mac pizza is sure to make your taste buds melt! Crafted with kneeded dough, freshly grated cheeses, and creamy sauce, it will make you say mmmmmm.

If you want a dish that will knock your socks off, look no further because you found it! Continue below for my recipe for mac and cheese pizza.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Pasta
  • Cheese
  • Milk
  • White Sauce

Steps

  1. Prepare dough by adding flour, yeast, sugar, and salt. Knead into a sticky dough
  2. Cook pasta as per box direcitons until soft
  3. Prepare cheesy sauce on warm stove top
  4. Add sauce to cooked pasta
  5. Spread dough to shape
  6. Top with white sauce and mac and cheese
  7. Bake for 30 min with oven set at 375 F
  8. Slice and enjoy!
